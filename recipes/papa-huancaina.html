Papa a la Huancaina

Description

Papa a la Huancaina, (potatoes Huancayo style) are boiled yellow potatoes covered with an even yellower spicy and creamy cheese sauce, and accompanied by hard-boiled eggs and black olives. This easy recipe is so popular that you can find the Huancaina sauce everywhere in Peru: as a dip for crudites, quail eggs, corn, or fried yucca, as a sauce for spaghetti or risotto, over Causa, with steak, you name it.
